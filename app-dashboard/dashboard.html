Welcome to FBF

Welcome to the InaSAFE Forcast Based Financing dashboard. This site is our first prototype implementation for a forecasted flood impact dashboard.

To get started, use the green flood selector icon below to select a date (valid dates have circles around them) for a historical or forecast flood event.

This site is developed in collaboration with the Red Cross Red Crescent Climate Center with funding from the World Bank GFDRR Challenge Fund.

Click the download button on the top right of the flood summary to download the flood data.
Click the Add flood button on the right side of the Nav bar to Add your flood layer.
Click the Browse Flood button on the bottom right of InaSAFE FBF to Browse Floods.
